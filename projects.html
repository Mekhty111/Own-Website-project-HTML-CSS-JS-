Calculator App (Java/Kotlin)

At a time when the play market did not have a large number of competing calculators, I had the idea to develop a calculator that could compete in design and convenience not only with calculators from the play market, but also with the one built into android

Here is my prototype of calculator app. Prototype was created and implemented from the Figma into Android Studio's view.

a dark theme for the application was also implemented, and high-quality and smooth animations were added

in the end, the result was a fairly high-quality application that to this day I consider a worthy competitor
